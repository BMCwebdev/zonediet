No Bean Turkey Chili

Other vegetables are used instead of beans.

Yield:
4 Servings
Preparation Time:
1 hour

Ingredients:

12 oz
Ground turkey breast
1 tbsp
Olive oil
3/4 cup
Celery - chopped
3
Green bell peppers - chopped
2 cups
Onion - chopped/dried
5 cups
Mushroom - chopped
1/2 cup
Fresh parsley - chopped, or cilantro
1 packet
El Paso chili seasoning mix
1 (14.5 oz) can
Diced tomatoes - with juice
15 oz
Tomato sauce
8 oz
Tomato puree
2 tbsps
Extra virgin olive oil - drizzle

Instructions:

Brown turkey in 1 tbsp oil on medium heat. Combine remaining ingredients except the rest of the extra virgin olive oil. If lacking liquid, add water in Â¼ cup increments. Cook an hour or longer. Divide into 4 serving bowls and stir 1 1/2 teaspoons of extra virgin olive oil into each. Submitted by: Nicole P.

Nutritional facts per serving: Calories 344; Protein 28g; Total Fat 12g; Carb. 35g; Fiber 8g
